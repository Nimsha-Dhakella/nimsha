About Us

JANGAON Institute of Pharmaceutical Sciences is located in a serene and calm campus of Yesvanthapur Village. Located 90 kms from Hyderabad and 50 kms from Warangal (Telangana), the college is spread over an area of 8 acres of land. The institute is affiliated to Kakatiya University and is approved by the Pharmacy council of India (PCI). The college offers 4-year undergraduate and 2- year postgraduate degree programme in Pharmaceutical science. The college, established in 2004, was set up with a vision to uplift and fulfill the academic requirement of deprived but deserving students of low.

Sythesis of Aspirin

The synthesis of aspirin is a straightforward experiment involving the reaction of salicylic acid with acetic anhydride, catalyzed by sulfuric acid. It demonstrates key concepts in organic chemistry, including reaction mechanisms and crystallization. The process is efficient, producing solid aspirin crystals, which can be purified and tested for quality. Overall, it’s an essential experiment for understanding pharmaceutical chemistry.

B.pharmacy

The duration of this course is 4 years. The B. Pharm is one of the popular job oriented course among the science students after class 12th. In this course the students study about the drugs and medicines, Pharmaceutical Engineering, Medicinal Chemistry etc.

D.pharmacy

The D Pharm course duration is two years duration, divided into four semesters that focus on pharmaceutical sciences, drug manufacturing, dispensing, and related areas. The course curriculum typically covers a wide range of subjects related to pharmacy and medicine.

Pharmaceutics

There is a lot of career scope in pharmaceutical science with the right qualifications. The pharma industry is expanding at a significant rate, creating new opportunities in the field for skilled professionals.

The terms 'pharmaceuticals' and 'pharmaceutical drugs' encompass a variety of medicines used to prevent, diagnose, treat or cure disease. The pharmaceutical drugs section of the NADK addresses a subsection of these medicines, namely those with analgesic or sedative-hypnotic properties.

Industrial pharmacy

The industrial pharmacy involves the pharmaceutical industry and includes the research, production, packaging, quality control, marketing and sales of pharmaceutical goods.

As an industrial pharmacist, you research and design medicine. This usually takes place in an industrial laboratory, and on an average day, you may help conduct clinical trials on a patient, use technology to synthesize new chemicals, and create a suggested course of treatment for a new pharmaceutical.
